# AccountaBuddy

## Title
A Mobile App to keep groups of friends accountable with their goals with goal tracking and streak building!

## Project Description
AccountaBuddy is a fitness app that transforms individual workouts into shared journeys by connecting friend.
We solve the consistency problem that trouble many fitness enthusiasts who have the desire but struggle with motivation.
Group accountability provides this motivation that a friend group needs to achieve their fitness goals.
We harness the power of accountability with your buddies to encourage everyone to stay committed.
